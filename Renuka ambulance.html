About Renuka Ambulance Service

Established with a mission to save lives, Renuka Ambulance Service has been providing reliable emergency medical transportation in Pune and surrounding areas for over a decade.

Our team of dedicated professionals includes trained paramedics, emergency medical technicians, and drivers who are committed to delivering prompt and compassionate care during medical emergencies.

We operate a fleet of well-equipped ambulances with advanced life support systems, ensuring that patients receive critical care during transit to healthcare facilities.
